Why should I choose WDT Property?

Not only can you buy properties from WDT Property, you can also make money from us. When you buy a new property, we offer you with package deals, such as offering people with a customized lease contract for your units, , so that you can earn rental and make a profit. Moreover, we will help you to maintain the property throughout the contract period including all the maintenance fees. We provide various types of high-quality real estate for your consideration. We have professional agents and customer services that can assist you to take the right property that suits your needs and requirements from purchase, lease and investment.


How do I buy/rent a unit from WDT Property?

You can contact us and tell us your requirements, preferences and considerations, so that we can provide you with recommendations of units or properties that are suitable for you from your aspects. You can first browse our website and once you have satisfying target, contact our customer service and we will provide you an agent to follow up immediately. Otherwise, you can contact our customer service directly and let us screen your ideal real estate options for you. After filtering from your preferences, we will send/display to you the available choices to you.


Should I sell my current property before buying a new property?

Most people sell their current property before buying a new house, but selling before buying is a risky decision, however, it also has great advantages. At WDT Property, you have 2 options:

  • Let us manage your current property for leasing as an investment where you can gain a profit.
