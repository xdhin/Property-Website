Organisational Structure




Description of Organisational Chart

WDT Property was founded in 2013 by Mr. Alvin Ng with the objective of making everyone capable to own their ideal and dream real estate and gain profits from it as a long-term investment.
Ms. Jenny Lim is an experienced president and chief executive officer who has inherited Mr. Alvin's determination and insistence in assisting clients to get insight into real estate knowledge that changes over time. She leads the team in achieving the company’s goals and objectives through unceasing innovation, to better adapt the social changes.
Mrs. Michelle, Mr. Samuel, Mr. Newt and Mr. Danny are good subordinates who are assisting Ms. Jenny in the company's business model and daily operations. Their joint efforts have formulated the company's clear growth strategy and provided huge opportunities for expanding potential market of WDT Property.
Among them, Mrs. Michelle is also in charge of the HR department, which responsible for the deployment and distribution of personnel management, as well as recruiting market talents.
Mr. Samuel is managing the company’s business marketplace in the property portal. He works with the agents and customer service departments together making WDT Property a popular and famous choice of consumers when investing in real estate.
In terms of fund management, Mr. Newt has many years of experience in handling and dividing funds in real estate investment, and supports the company’s operations.
Mr. Danny has gone through WDT Property years of operation. He has been providing his expert knowledge in offering assistance to the board from managing personnel resources to marketing. He strengthens the board’s capability in directing the company’s growth and by offering innovative advice and dynamic perspectives.
With the efforts and pursuit of the board of directors, the growth stage of WDT Property has continued to improve in recent years to provide customers with better services.
