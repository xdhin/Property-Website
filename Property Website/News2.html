Volunteer Program at PAWS


We felt appreciate and thank PAWS, for giving us the occasion to get along with the lovely and furry pals. Our company had a very pleasant time, walking, feeding and bathing the dogs. WDT Property has donated a total of RM30,000 to the "Let's Adopt, Don't Abandon" corporate social responsibility activities. We hope that this small donation can help the shelter to reduce the burden of expenses.
