Company Description

Welcome to WDT Property for real estate shopping in Malaysia. We are committed on providing clients with the resources they need to make better-informed property decisions. We help our clients recognize the property market by combining property listings with the latest news, housing price data and local information.

WDT Property was established to democratize property investment. We are keen to make the market more effective for property clients by combining the free real-time value estimation of all Malaysian real estates with selling price, local market information and thousands of real estate available for sale and rental. Our features enable users to gain insight into the market and discover new trendy information making WDT Property the choice for property clients looking for real estate and do their market research.
